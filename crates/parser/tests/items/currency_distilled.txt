Item Class: Stackable Currency
Rarity: Currency
Liquid Envy
--------
Stack Size: 8/10
--------
Removes a random modifer and Augments a Rare Basic Jewel with a
new guaranteed Crafted modifier


Sapphire: (5-15)% increased Spell Damage
Ruby: (4-6)% of Skill Mana Costs Converted to Life Costs
Emerald: (3-5)% increased Cooldown Recovery Rate
--------
Can be used at The Withered Willow to Instil Amulets with a Notable Passive Skill.
Right click this item then left click a Rare Jewel to apply it.
Shift click to unstack.
